Free Energy Generation Breakthrough

The Windelectric Magnetic Power Generators (WMPG) provides access to an inexpensive, continuously available, and non-polluting method for generating electrical energy with scalability to small, medium and large-scale applications. WMPG produces reliable electricity non-stop without requiring any fuel input; WMPG are completely hassle free. WMPG is driven by permanent magnets built on the rotor and control circuits and requires no external fossil fuels, nuclear fuels or hydro input.
