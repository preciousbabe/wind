Windelectric is the leader in the new energy innovation, changing the system of
electrical energy generation and efficiency.

Windelectric is a credible Nigerian company based in Ogun state, Nigeria and its team is comprised of individuals with wide experience in power generation technologies. We have explored the open source journals of one of the greatest inventors of all time, Nicola Tesla to design & model the Windelectric Magnetic Power Generator (WMPG) .

Redefining Electrical Power Generation WMPG is an electrical generation system based on magnetic levitation technology. It is equipped with a small battery to initiate rotation of the generator and supporting electronic system operation, no external input power required, thus power generation is realized at extremely low rotation per minute (RPM).
